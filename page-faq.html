FAQ

  • Are silicone accessories safe for my baby to use?
    Yes, silicone accessories are generally considered safe for babies. They are non-toxic, BPA-free, and free from harmful chemicals.
  • How do silicone accessories help with teething pain?
    Silicone accessories provide a soft and soothing texture for babies to chew on, which helps alleviate teething pain by providing gentle pressure and relief to their sore gums.
  • What age group are silicone accessories suitable for?
    Silicone accessories are typically designed for babies and can be used from newborns up to around three years old, depending on the specific product.
  • How do personalized silicone accessories make a unique gift for a baby?
    By customizing silicone accessories with names, favorite colors, or cute designs, they become special and sentimental gifts that can be cherished as keepsakes.
  • How do silicone accessories enhance color perception in babies?
    The bright and eye-catching colors of silicone accessories can stimulate a baby's visual development, helping improve their color perception and recognition skills.
  • Can silicone accessories help babies with their motor skill development?
    Absolutely! Silicone accessories, such as teething bracelets or sensory toys, encourage babies to explore and manipulate objects, aiding in the development of their fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination.
  • Can silicone accessories be used as sensory toys?
    Yes, the different textures and shapes of silicone accessories can provide a sensory experience for babies, stimulating their touch and sensory exploration.
  • Are silicone accessories suitable for babies with allergies or sensitivities?
    Silicone is hypoallergenic and generally well-tolerated by babies with allergies or sensitivities.
  • Do silicone accessories have any additional benefits apart from teething relief?
    Yes, silicone accessories can serve various purposes. They can be used as soothing toys, provide tactile stimulation, and aid in the development of oral skills.
  • Can silicone accessories be refrigerated or frozen to provide extra relief for teething babies?
    Yes, many silicone accessories can be safely refrigerated or frozen, which helps provide additional cooling sensation and relief for teething babies.
  • Are silicone accessories designed to be easily cleaned on the go?
    Many silicone accessories are designed with convenience in mind and can be easily wiped clean with a cloth or baby wipes while you're out and about.
  • Are silicone accessories odorless and tasteless?
    Silicone is known for being odorless and tasteless, making it a safe and neutral material for babies to interact with during feeding or teething.
  • Do silicone accessories contain any latex or phthalates?
    No, silicone accessories are latex-free and typically free from phthalates and other harmful chemicals, making them safe for babies with latex allergies.

Usage instructions & Precautions

To make the most of your silicone accessories for children, please follow these instructions:
1. Fixing the Pacifier or Teethe
  • Using the provided loop, securely attach the pacifier or teether to the silicone holder
  • Ensure the item is tightly fastened to prevent any accidental detachment
2. Attaching to Baby's Clothes or Stroller
  • Once the pacifier or teether is secured, use the clip to attach the holder to your baby's clothing or stroller
  • Ensure a firm grip to prevent the holder from coming loose and potentially falling
Care Method
  • Before the initial use, it is highly recommended to perform an antibacterial treatment using baby wet wipes. This helps maintain hygiene and cleanliness
  • In case of heavy contamination, rinse the silicone holder with warm water
  • Avoid washing the silicone accessories in sterilizers or dishwashers, as these may cause damage to the products or compromise their performance
  • Please avoid wetting the wooden clip as it may cause damage
Attention: To ensure the durability of your silicone accessories, it's crucial to protect the metal and wooden elements from moisture. Avoid exposing them to excessive water or damp environments

Precautions

Always use the holder under adult supervision to prevent any potential hazards
Before every use, carefully inspect the product to ensure its integrity
Do not use the holder if any signs of damage, such as cracks or tears, are detected
