About us

Let me tell you the story of Lucky Baby, a brand of silicone baby accessories for the tiniest munchkins, created by none other than yours truly

My main goal was to provide relief from teething pain and support the development of motor skills in babies. It all started when I crafted the very first pacifier holder for my own little one and boy, did it work like magic! That success propelled me to share my creation with other parents, and Lucky Baby quickly became a hit among parents in the UAE. Now, Lucky Baby continues to bring smiles, comfort, and ease to little ones and their families.
